# Target Companies — Direct Apply

> Companies that build on **React / Next.js / Node** and align with your interests: **anime, games, and
> fitness/gym**. The play (per the "Buddha Depp" lesson): pick companies you'd genuinely love to work
> for, watch their boards, and apply directly instead of only fishing in the big aggregators.

## How to read this list

- **🌎 Hires globally / LATAM-friendly** — realistically open to a Costa Rica remote contractor.
- **🇺🇸 US-leaning** — great companies, but often need US work auth or are hybrid. Apply anyway if a
  listing says "remote — anywhere," but verify per role. If they won't hire CR directly, they may
  still reach you through Turing / Wellfound / Hired.
- **Apply direct** = go to the career page, no platform pool.

Stacks below are based on public job posts / engineering blogs (June 2026) — **always confirm on the live listing**.

### ✈️ Open to relocation? Then ignore the 🇺🇸 flags as blockers

The reach flags assume **remote-from-Costa-Rica**. If you'll relocate, the 🇺🇸 "stretches" become
normal applications — most large companies here sponsor visas. Two realistic routes:

- **🇺🇸 USA** — H-1B is a lottery (file Q1, capped), but **O-1** ("extraordinary ability" — strong for
  a senior dev with a public portfolio + open-source) and **L-1** (transfer after a year at a global
  employer) are non-lottery. Crunchyroll, Discord, WHOOP, Reddit, and the AI labs all sponsor.
- **🇪🇺 / 🇬🇧 / 🇨🇦 — much easier than the US.** Canada (Global Talent Stream / Express Entry),
  UK (Global Talent / Skilled Worker visa), Germany/Netherlands/Portugal (EU Blue Card). **Vercel,
  Spotify, GitLab, Gymshark, Voodoo, ElevenLabs** hire and relocate into Europe routinely; these are
  your highest-probability relocation targets.

**Filter tip:** on a listing, look for "visa sponsorship available" / "relocation assistance" — and
just *apply* even when it's blank; for a senior React/Node/Next dev, companies often open it case-by-case.

## 🎯 Application playbook

1. **Tier your targets.** Realistic-reach remote-first ones — **Bitso, Kraken, Consensys, Vercel,
   Hugging Face, Shopify, GitLab, Automattic, Wildlife, Voodoo, Gymshark, Hevy** — get tailored
   applications. Stretch/US-leaning ones (Crunchyroll, Discord, Twitch, WHOOP, Strava, OpenAI,
   Anthropic, Meta) get a clean apply + a recruiter connect on LinkedIn.
2. **Bookmark the boards, not just the jobs.** Most of these post on **Greenhouse / Lever / Ashby** —
   you can set up alerts on each. Check weekly.
3. **Two paths into US-leaning ones:** apply direct *and* make sure your Turing / Wellfound / Hired
   profiles are sharp, because that's how these same companies often reach LATAM contractors.
4. **Re-apply is fine.** A first-round rejection (e.g. Vuori) doesn't lock you out — different role,
   6+ months later, stronger portfolio.
5. **Lead with the stack-match.** Your React + Node + Next + TS + GraphQL profile is a direct hit for
   Discord, Crunchyroll, Wildlife, and most fitness apps — say so in the first line of your application.
